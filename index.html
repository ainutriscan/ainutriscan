Why AI NutriScan

Built for people who
actually care what they eat

From students to professional athletes — AI NutriScan gives everyone the nutritional intelligence they deserve, powered by the latest AI vision technology.

📷

Instant Photo Scanning

Snap a photo of any meal — home-cooked, restaurant, or street food — and get a complete nutrient breakdown in under 3 seconds. Recognises thousands of local and international dishes.

📊

Full Macro Dashboard

Track calories, protein, carbohydrates, fat, fibre, vitamins, and minerals all in one clean dashboard. Understand your nutritional balance across days and weeks.

🤖

AI Nutrition Insights

Get personalised recommendations based on your goals, dietary preferences, and health history. Our AI learns your habits and suggests smarter food choices every day.

🌾

Farm & Ingredient Tracing

Know where your food comes from. AI NutriScan partners with local farmers to provide full ingredient traceability — from field to plate — with freshness and sustainability scores.

🍽️

Restaurant Menu Scanner

Eating out? Scan the menu or your dish at partner restaurants and instantly see the nutritional value of everything you order. No guesswork — just full clarity.

🎓

Campus Nutrition Programme

Special partnerships with universities to bring AI NutriScan to student canteens, health programmes, and nutrition research labs, supporting healthier campus communities.

How It Works

Three steps to smarter eating

No spreadsheets. No manual entry. Just point, scan, and understand your food in real time.

1

📷 Snap Your Food

Open the AI NutriScan app and photograph your meal — or upload from your gallery. Works with any food, any plate size, under any lighting condition.

2

🤖 AI Analyses Instantly

Our computer vision AI identifies every ingredient on your plate, estimates portions with precision, and calculates full macros and micronutrients in under 3 seconds.

3

📊 Get Smart Insights

View your complete nutritional breakdown. Track daily and weekly trends. Receive personalised recommendations to help you hit your goals — weight loss, muscle gain, or simply eating healthier.

4

🔄 Build Better Habits

AI NutriScan learns your eating patterns over time and proactively nudges you toward better choices — without being preachy or restrictive.
