How It Works

Four steps to smarter eating

No spreadsheets, no manual entry. Point, scan, and understand your food in real time.

01

Snap your food

Open the app and photograph your meal — or upload from your gallery. Works with any food, any plate, any lighting.

02

AI analyses instantly

Our computer vision model identifies every ingredient, estimates portions with precision, and calculates full macros in under 3 seconds.

03

Get smart insights

View your complete nutritional breakdown. Track daily and weekly trends. Receive personalised recommendations for your goals.

04

Build better habits

AI NutriScan learns your eating patterns over time and proactively nudges you toward smarter choices — without being restrictive.
